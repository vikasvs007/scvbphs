Learning Approach

SCV & BPHS has implemented leading teaching methodologies for the best outcome. The STEAM structure explains how all the divisions of education and life work together.

STEAM = Science & Technology interpreted through Engineering & the Arts, all based in Mathematical elements.

It is a flexible interdisciplinary framework promoting a natural approach to learning. With problem-solving, fearlessness, critical thinking and making skills at its core, STEAM is not just a program but a philosophy at School Of India.
